Good questions

Frequently asked

Everything homeowners usually want to know before booking a painter in Las Vegas.

Do you offer free estimates?
Yes, always free and no obligation. We come out, look at the project, and give you a clear, written quote. There's never a fee just to get a number.
What areas do you serve?
The entire Las Vegas Valley: Las Vegas, North Las Vegas, Henderson, Summerlin, Enterprise, and the surrounding communities. Not sure if you're in range? Just call and ask.
How long will my project take?
Most homes are finished in days, not weeks. A full 5-bedroom interior, high ceilings included, is often done in just 2 to 3 days. We'll give you a realistic timeline with your estimate and stick to it.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Lara Painting Pro is licensed and insured, and we've been a Thumbtack Top Pro in both 2024 and 2025. You're covered, and the work is backed by a 4.9-star track record across 179 reviews.
What kind of paint do you use?
Premium, long-lasting products from Sherwin-Williams and Dunn-Edwards. We'll help you pick the right colors and finishes for each room and for the Las Vegas climate.
Do you handle prep and repairs first?
Always. Great paint starts with great prep: patching holes, drywall and stucco repair, popcorn-texture removal, caulking, and protecting your floors and furniture before a single drop of paint goes on.
Can you paint kitchen cabinets?
Yes, cabinet refinishing is one of our specialties. It transforms a kitchen for a fraction of the cost of a remodel, with a smooth, durable, factory-grade finish.
Do you do both homes and businesses?
We do residential, commercial, and new construction. Offices, retail, and rentals included, scheduled to keep disruption to a minimum.
How do I pay, and when?
We accept cash, check, credit card, and Zelle. We'll walk the finished job with you first. It's not done until you're happy with every edge.
